Note: Normal document flow, but offset relative to itself with positioning propertites: top, right, bottom, left. The offset does not affect the position of any other elements, therefore its as as if the element takes us its original space.

Example: Absolute Square

Note: Element is removed from document flow, and no space is created for the element in the page layout. It is positioned relative to its closest positioned ancestor, if any; otherwise, it is placed relative to the initial containing block. Its final position is determined by the values of top, right, bottom, and left.

Note: The green square is relative to body! Its all the way up there.

Creates new stacking context.
